Specialized Leasing Company is committed to maintaining continuous communication with customers to address their complaints and grievances. To ensure customers' right to file a complaint, an effective system has been established and is managed by the company's dedicated complaints unit. Therefore, any person wishing to file a complaint has the right to communicate directly with the Complaints Unit through the following channels:

  • Personal visit to the headquarters building.
  • Using the website by filling out the adjacent form.
  • Company's social media platforms.
  • Sending an email to the Complaints Unit at: QualityAssurance@hbtf.com.jo

Please be informed that upon receiving any complaint, we will work on processing it and making the appropriate decision within a maximum of ten working days from the date of receiving the complaint and all related documents.

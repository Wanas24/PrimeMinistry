About SLCJO

Overview

The Specialized Leasing Company is a private shareholding company founded by the Housing Bank for Trade and Finance in 30.5.2005, and proceeded its activities in 2006. It currently holds a capital of 30 million Jordanian Dinars.

Our Vision

Introducing financial leasing, customizing solutions, and staying current on market trends to provide high-quality innovative financial services.

Our Mission

To position the Specialized Leasing Company as a leader in offering leasing solutions to targeted business sectors.

Our Objectives

  • To offer innovative financial solutions for clients.
  • Revamping financing concepts with diverse alternatives.
